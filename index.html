Why this matters A viral coefficient of 0% means every new customer costs CAC. A coefficient of 10% means one in ten active customers brings a new one, for free, every month. At scale, this is the difference between a business that plateaus when ad spend caps out and one that compounds. Adjust the lever and watch the gap on the chart above widen.

Strategic read Unit economics are a portfolio question, not a single number. The blended ratio can look healthy while one or two channels burn cash. Use channel-level LTV:CAC to reallocate spend, not just to grade.
