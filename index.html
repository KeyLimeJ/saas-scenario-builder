Strategic read Unit economics are a portfolio question, not a single number. The blended ratio can look healthy while one or two channels burn cash. Use channel-level LTV:CAC to reallocate spend, not just to grade.
Models in production Four production models feed every metric in this app. Toggle ML · ON/OFF in the sidebar footer to swap between flat assumptions and model-driven inputs across every view. The pipeline is DBT for features, Python (XGBoost, lifetimes, Prophet, scikit-learn) for training, Snowflake Python UDFs for serving, and Looker for exposure.
